Wine Quality Analysis

Hypothesis

Through comprehensive analysis of wine characteristics, we aim to identify key factors that contribute to wine quality ratings and understand the relationships between various wine attributes such as acidity, alcohol content, and overall consumer preferences.

Project Overview

This analysis explores various wine datasets to uncover patterns and relationships between different characteristics of wines and their quality ratings. By examining factors such as acidity levels, alcohol content, and flavor compounds, we seek to understand what makes a wine receive higher ratings from experts and consumers alike.

Our visualization tools allow for interactive exploration of these relationships, helping to identify trends and patterns across different wine varieties and regions. This analysis can provide valuable insights for both wine enthusiasts and industry professionals.
